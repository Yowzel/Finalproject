Imminent Reprisal

DESCRIPTION

The award winning Imminent Reprisal is an online choose-your-own adventure game where the player guides the protagonist through a number of difficult choices which affect the outcome of the story. The protagonist is a young adult who lives in Nysa, a region where Dionysus, god of Wine, is worshipped. Provoked by the flashing images of a child, our hero sets out on a challenging journey to find wisdom and restore balance to Nysa, which has become permeated by drunken disorder.
